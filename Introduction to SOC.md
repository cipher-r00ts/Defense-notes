# What is SOC
- SOC is Security Operations Center 
- It is a place where information security team continuously monitors and analyzes the security of an organization
- The primary purpose of the SOC
> [!note] Detect, Analyze and respond to cybersecurity incidents.

# Types of SOC models
There are several type of SOC models depending on the needs of the user 
![[Pasted image 20260706091121.png]]

## In-house SOC 
This team is formed when organization build its cyber security team. 

## Virtual SOC
A SOC team working remotely 

## Co-managed SOC
Internal SOC + external managed Security Service Provider (MSSP)

## Command SOC
A SOC team overseeing smaller SOC operations in a larger region

# A successful SOC needs strong relation between people, process and technology

## People 

A strong SOC teams needs highly qualified personnel who are familiar with security alerts and attack scenarios. Since, the type of attacks keep on changing a SOC team should also adapt to new attack types and are willing to put efforts in researching about it

## Processes
To develop a good SOC you need to align it with different security requirements, such as NIST, PCI, HIPAA 

## Technology
The team needs to have different products for many tasks, such as penetration testing, detection, prevention, and analysis, and they need to follow the market and technology closely to find the best solution for the organization. Sometimes the best product on the market may not be the best product for your team. Remember to consider other factors such as the organization's budget.
